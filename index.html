About

I started my programming journey a few years ago. Diving into the free to use engines such as Unity and Unreal both of which have rekindled my drive to learn and create. Along with making games I have began looking into web development learning and using Front-End languages to create web pages. I strive to learn all I can about the process it takes to make games and web apps focusing on how I can become better at what I do.

This has given me an appreciation for the amount of work and effort it takes to come out with even the simplest projects let alone the large and complex ones. My desire to create keeps me hungry for the knowledge I need to do my very best. I enjoy challenges since they push me to be better and I find that I learn more from my failures than I do from my successes.

I am very enthusiastice about creating content and visibly apealing websites. When I get stuck on a problem I seek out the information from anywhere possible. I am a team player and more then willing to help those around me. Constructive criticism is received like a professional, knowing that it is being given to make the project or team better.

Description

The Objective of this game is for the player to prevent all of their cows from being abducted for as long as possible.  The player receives points for each UFO destroyed, their score is then saved and stored after all the cows are abducted.
